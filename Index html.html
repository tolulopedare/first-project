About Tolu !

I got the inspiration to learn coding ever since i watched Jeniffer Opal, her Coding beginner story was really inspiring.

Her first experience and being one of the Black women in Tech to be a web developer really motivited me to learn.

I have always doubted myself about coding, but right from the moment i watched her story, i felt like i can do this.

She made me aware that 'Coding for girls' was providing a free course, she supported and encouraged me to learn

I must say, i have only started learning how to code but i am really enjoying it.

Thank you to Jennifer and thanks to all my Course Tutors.
